Mike & Stephanie

October 5, 2018

The Couple



Mike grew up in Chicopee, Massachusetts with his parents, Michael & Youngsook Rogers. Stephanie grew up in Carlisle, New York with her parents, Michael & Marijeanne Broadwell, and her four sisters. Mike attended Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI), while Stephanie attended Russell Sage College, both in Downtown Troy, NY. They met in 2012, at one of Mike's fraternity's events and began dating shortly after. Mike graduated from RPI in 2015, and accepted a job offer at Ford Motor Company in Southeast Michigan. After graduating from Russell Sage in 2016, Stephanie moved to Michigan to be with Mike. The happy couple lives in St. Clair Shores, MI with their dog, King Charles. Mike works as a Carrier Planner in Ford's Connected Vehicle Carrier Planning department, while Stephanie works as a Back-end Developer at Signs365.

Mike and Stephanie are excited to share their wedding day with all of you.
