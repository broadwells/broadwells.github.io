About

I'm originally from Upstate New York, near the Albany-area. I moved to the Detroit Area in May of 2016 after completing my undergrad with a Bachelor of Science degree in Nutrition. After moving to Michigan, and being surrounded by the Big Three and other Detroit based companies, I realized how much the technology world is ever changing and growing and how I want to be apart of that experience. I came across Grand Circus' website and after some research, I decided learning Back-End development was what I wanted and applied for the Java April 2017 Bootcamp. The bootcamp has taught me a lot about coding and how to be an efficient developer. I am looking for a career in Software Development that will allow me to use the skills that I have acquired from Grand Circus, but will also provide me the resources to add and improve upon my skills.
